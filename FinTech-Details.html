Course Description

Fintech is not only a latest buzz word, but a fundamental change poised to bring not only new methods of working in the financial industry but morph some of the fundamentals of the industry. This course introduces participants to basic concepts of Fintech, Big Data, Distributed ledgers, Cryptocurrency, Process Automation, and formulation of digital strategies.

This course is designed to introduce some of the basic concepts and uses of Financial technology in the financial business services sector. Participants do not need to belong to the banking sector but should have fundamental knowledge of how the industry works and its impact on consumers.
